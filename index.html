Questions, answered plainly

Is this legal advice?

No. LeaseCodex produces review-ready administrative summaries for human review. It does not interpret leases, replace counsel, or provide legal advice. Always verify against the signed lease and consult your attorney for legal questions.

What documents does it handle?

Commercial leases and amendments as PDFs — including scanned documents. Office, retail, industrial, and mixed-use.

How accurate is it?

Every field carries a confidence flag, and every value links to its source page so you can verify it in seconds. Nothing enters your tracker unreviewed — that's the point.

Where does my data go?

Your documents stay private and encrypted, are never used to train shared models, and can be deleted at any time.
